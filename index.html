#METOO

MeToo is a movement born on Twitter in October 2017 that denounces harassment, sexual assault and rape and calls for breaking the law of silence in Hollywood. In response to the Weinstein case, actress Alyssa Milano revived the #MeToo hashtag that appeared ten years earlier on MySpace. This first tweet from Alyssa Milano created a worldwide sensation. Very quickly, famous women and men tweeted "Me too" and shared their stories. The next day Twitter confirms that #MeToo has been tweeted more than half a million times. There were a lot of reactions on Twitter, both supportive and hateful, as presented below.

263 993 supportive tweets

36 007 hateful tweets
